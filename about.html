About Me

Arizona based UX/UI/Graphic Designer. I'm always up for a challenge!

Hi! I’m Peggy, a graphic designer and recent University of Arizona UX/UI Bootcamp graduate. Skilled in visual design, I love to take my work a step further by innovating ideal user experiences. Proficient in Illustrator, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and WordPress as well as conducting user research and testing, wireframing and prototyping, I deliver engaging design solutions from concept to final polished product.

Currently I work with entrepreneurs to actualize their dreams from the ground up, from business branding all the way through to web design. I find a great deal of satisfaction from helping others to create a tangible business from nothing but thoughts and ideas.

When not playing with pixels or digging deeper into user psychology I can be found outdoors exploring with my dog, trying to take the perfect photo, or in the kitchen “innovating” dinner!
